If ordering online, you can call the outlet number displayed in the mobile app to know if you can get your order free if not delivered within 1 hour.

How do I get a free pizza from Domino’s?

You can get a free pizza from Domino’s if your home delivery order doesn’t reach your destination within the stipulated time. However, this rule might not apply during rush hours or during the festive season. But you can always use Domino’s coupons to reduce your bill value or even get some side menu or dessert at a discounted value.

How much is a 12-inch pizza from Domino’s?

The price of the pizza will depend on your pick of the pizza from the signature, supreme, or standard range. What is the most popular Domino's pizza?
